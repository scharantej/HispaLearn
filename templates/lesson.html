Lesson {{ lesson_id }}: Basic Greetings

In this lesson, you will learn how to greet people in Spanish. You will also learn how to introduce yourself and ask others how they are doing.

Greetings

  • Hola - Hello
  • Buenos días - Good morning
  • Buenas tardes - Good afternoon
  • Buenas noches - Good evening

Introductions

  • Me llamo... - My name is...
  • ¿Cómo te llamas? - What is your name?
  • ¿Cómo estás? - How are you?
  • Estoy bien, gracias. - I am fine, thank you.

Practice

Try using the phrases you have learned to greet someone and introduce yourself. You can practice with a friend or family member, or you can use the online resources provided below.
